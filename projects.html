Bitcoin Price Prediction Using RNN/LSTM

A deep learning model utilizing Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks for Bitcoin price prediction leverages the strengths of these architectures in capturing temporal dependencies in time-series data. Bitcoin's price is highly volatile and influenced by complex patterns, making traditional forecasting techniques less effective. RNNs are capable of handling sequential data, while LSTMs and hyperparameter tuning, can significantly enhance the accuracy of the predictions, making it a robust method for forecasting Bitcoin's price movements.
